Natalie E. Sautter graduated with honors from the University of Florida in December 2008. She earned her Bachelor of Science degree in public relations, a minor in leadership, and an outside concentration in psychology. She is currently pursuing her Master of Arts degree in mass communication with a specialization in public relations from the University of Florida.

Ms. Sautter’s passions include public relations, communications, event planning, critical thinking, design, and customer service. She has years of experience and has also completed numerous internships, done freelance work, and volunteered her services for several local non-profits in Gainesville, Fla.
